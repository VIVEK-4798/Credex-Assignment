## FAQ Section

### Question 1: "Do I need to create an account?"
**Answer**: Nope. Just enter your tools and get recommendations. We only store your audit if you choose to share it. Premium features (like audit history) require an optional email.

### Question 2: "How accurate are the recommendations?"
**Answer**: Very. We use current pricing data and confidence scoring. Each recommendation includes implementation difficulty, so you can judge feasibility. We never exaggerate savings.

### Question 3: "Will you spam my email?"
**Answer**: Only if you sign up for premium. We never sell or share your data. Privacy is core to our design.

### Question 4: "Can I share this with my team?"
**Answer**: Absolutely. After your audit, we give you a public URL you can share with finance, leadership, or engineering. No one needs to log in to view it.

### Question 5: "What if my tool isn't on your list?"
**Answer**: We cover 20+ major AI tools. If yours is missing, let us know and we'll add it. You can also enter custom tools with your spend.

### Question 6: "How is this different from ChatGPT or Perplexity?"
**Answer**: We're not a chat tool. We're an audit tool. We specialize in analyzing your *entire* AI tool portfolio and identifying overlap, waste, and optimization opportunities.

## Messaging Pillars

1. **Speed**: "In 2 minutes" is repeated across all messaging. Removes friction objection.

2. **Privacy**: "No email required" + "public URL" removes trust objection.

3. **Specificity**: Not "save money" but "$2K–$20K annually" + specific savings by tool.

4. **Confidence**: Confidence scoring + implementation difficulty > exaggerated claims.

5. **Simplicity**: "No login" + "no spreadsheets" + "free" removes complexity objection.
