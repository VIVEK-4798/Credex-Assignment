# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Positioning Statement

**For**: Finance teams and tech leads at 5–500 person companies
**Who need**: Visibility into AI tool spend and portfolio optimization
**The AI Spend Audit**: Is a free, no-login audit tool
**That**: Identifies cost savings and tool redundancies in seconds
**Unlike**: Spreadsheet audits and procurement platforms
**Because**: It's fast, shareable, and respects privacy

---

## Target Customer Personas

### Persona 1: Finance Manager (Primary)

**Name**: Jamie, Finance Manager at 50-person SaaS startup

**Background**:
- Responsible for controlling spend across engineering + operations
- Recently tasked with auditing "random AI tool charges"
- Uses spreadsheets + Expensify to track spend
- Frustrated that engineering teams don't coordinate tool purchases

**Pain**:
- "We have ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, some custom API spend... I have no idea how much we're actually spending"
- "Every time I ask what we're using, I get a different answer"
- "Finance leadership is pressuring me to show ROI on AI tools"

**Motivation**:
- Wants credible, fast audit to present to leadership
- Wants to stop questions about AI spend ("Here's an audit")
- Wants to identify quick wins ($5K+ savings)

**Buying behavior**:
- Tries free tools first
- Shares internally before considering paid
- Needs business case + ROI to justify premium tool

### Persona 2: Engineering Lead (Secondary)

**Name**: Alex, VP Engineering at 100-person company

**Background**:
- Responsible for engineering tool stack
- Didn't know other teams were using Claude/ChatGPT
- Wants to optimize team but doesn't know where to start

**Pain**:
- "We have overlap — 3 teams using ChatGPT, 2 using Claude, 1 using custom"
- "Can't convince teams to consolidate without data"
- "Need clear recommendations to present to other engineering leads"

**Motivation**:
- Wants data to back consolidation decision
- Wants to show engineering discipline to leadership
- Wants to free up budget for other investments

**Buying behavior**:
- Shares with peers ("Hey, this tool exists")
- Unlikely to pay (engineers don't like tools)
- Becomes evangelist if tool is useful

### Persona 3: CISO/Security Lead (Tertiary)

**Name**: Sam, CISO at 200-person company

**Background**:
- Concerned about shadow IT + unapproved tools
- Needs inventory of AI tools + access
- Wants audit trail + compliance

**Pain**:
- "I don't know what AI tools we're using or who has access"
- "We need to audit third-party AI access before SOC 2 compliance"
- "Need a quick report to share with auditors"

**Motivation**:
- Wants comprehensive tool inventory
- Wants compliance documentation
- Wants to reduce security risk from unauthorized tools

**Buying behavior**:
- Willing to pay for professional tool + support
- High LTV if solved
- Wants customization + reporting

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Freemium Model**:
```
Free tier: $0 ARPU, 70% of users
- Lead capture: 30% rate
- Lead → trial: 10% rate
- CAC: $0, Value: $0

Premium tier: $99/mo, 30% of users
- Churn: 5% MOM (95% retention)
- LTV: $99 × 12 ÷ 0.05 = $23,760
- CAC: $500 (blended)
- LTV:CAC: 47.5x ✅
```

**Enterprise Model**:
```
Enterprise: $5K–$20K/mo
- Contract length: 12 months
- LTV: $60K–$240K (12-month deals)
- CAC: $5,000–$10,000
- LTV:CAC: 6–48x ✅
```

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Larger Competitors Enter Market
**Mitigation**:
- Move fast to partnerships (establish moat before giants notice)
- Build community (network effects)
- Specialize in AI (depth > breadth)

### Risk 2: User Churn (No Repeat Audits)
**Mitigation**:
- Freemium model (premium for history)
- Quarterly "re-audit" emails
- Competitor benchmarking (gives repeat reason)

### Risk 3: Market Adoption Slower Than Expected
**Mitigation**:
- Pivot to "selling to vendors" (instead of end users)
- Partner with consultants (white-label)
- B2B2C through finance platforms

### Risk 4: Audit Quality Issues
**Mitigation**:
- Transparent rules (users see the logic)
- Feedback loop ("Did this help?")
- Customer support (quick feedback incorporation)
